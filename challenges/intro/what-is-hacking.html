What is hacking?

  • What is Hacking
  • Where to Go From Here

This website aims to teach you the basics of ethical hacking. But what is hacking? And why is it something you should learn?

Hacking is the process of finding loopholes (called exploits) in the rules of a system, and using them to achieve some kind of goal, often gaining unauthorized access.
Ethical Hacking is the process of finding those exploits not to attack the system, but to discover security flaws and protect the system. This is very common in the field of cyber security

Knowing how to hack is an invaluable skill in the 21st century, when so much of our lives are online or digital. If you know how to hack you know what kind of risks you face online, and what you can do to protect yourself and those around you. The cybersecurity field is also a thriving field that will only continue to grow as more and more of the world is digitized.
